Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 24, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to CalmRelaxFlow. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our application.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide a personalized experience:

  • Identity Data: Name and email address (provided via Google Login).
  • Usage Data: Meditation minutes and daily streaks stored locally on your device.
  • Journal Data: Reflections you write are stored only on your device (LocalStorage) and are not uploaded to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Personalize mindfulness recommendations using the Google Gemini API.
  • Track your meditation progress and streaks.
  • Find nearby meditation centers using Google Maps services.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google GenAI (Gemini): To generate mindfulness advice. No personal identifiers are sent to the AI.
  • Google Maps: To provide location-based zen center information.

5. Data Retention

Your journal entries and streaks are stored locally in your browser/app cache. If you clear your app data or browser cache, this information will be deleted. We do not maintain a remote database of your private reflections.
